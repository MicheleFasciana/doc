LOC_TE_FEEDBACK_FLAG
--------------------

**Definition**

Flag to signal electron temperature evolution

**Comments**

- LOC_TE_FEEDBACK_FLAG = 0 : Do not evolve :math:`T_e`
- LOC_TE_FEEDBACK_FLAG = 1 : Evolve :math:`T_e`

**Internal name**

loc_te_feedback_flag

**Default**

1
