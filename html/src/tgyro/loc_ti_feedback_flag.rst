LOC_TI_FEEDBACK_FLAG
--------------------

**Definition**

Flag to signal ion temperature evolution

**Comments**

- LOC_TI_FEEDBACK_FLAG = 0 : Do not evolve :math:`T_i`
- LOC_TI_FEEDBACK_FLAG = 1 : Evolve :math:`T_i`

**Internal name**

loc_ti_feedback_flag

**Default**

1
