LOC_HE_FEEDBACK_FLAG
--------------------

**Definition**

Flag to signal helium ash evolution

**Comments**

- LOC_HE_FEEDBACK_FLAG = 0 : Do not evolve ash`
- LOC_HE_FEEDBACK_FLAG = 1 : Evolve ash

**Internal name**

loc_he_feedback_flag

**Default**

0
