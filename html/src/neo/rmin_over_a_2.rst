RMIN_OVER_A_2

**Definition**

The ratio of the midplane minor radius :math:`r` to the normalizing length scale:math:`a`.

**Comments**
  
- For N_RADIAL > 1, this parameter is the upper bound of the radial grid.
- For N_RADIAL=1, this parameter is not used.

**Internal name**
  
rmin_2_in

**Default**

0.6
